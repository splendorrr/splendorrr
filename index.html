Tennis in Boulder?

Yes, please. With the closure of my second home Rocky Mountain Tennis Center, I wanted to build a tennis court where I can play...but also my friends. So, here it is :) Oh, also anyone is welcome. You don't have to be a member. You don't have to be my friend. But if you are coming to play here, it means you're cool and we can be friends. Jokes aside, I wish you a warm welcome and have fun.
